Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto blends ancient temples, serene gardens, and traditional streets with a vibrant modern culture—perfect for a calm yet inspiring getaway.

  1. Fushimi Inari Taisha — endless vermilion torii gates.
  2. Kiyomizu-dera — hilltop temple with sweeping views.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove — otherworldly bamboo paths.
  4. Gion district — historic lanes and teahouses.
  5. Nijō Castle — shogun residence with “nightingale floors.”
  • Kaiseki — seasonal multi-course dining.
  • Yudōfu — delicate tofu hot pot.
  • Matcha sweets — cakes, mochi, and parfaits.
  • Okonomiyaki — savory pancake Kyoto-style.
  • Yakitori — grilled skewers from street stalls.
