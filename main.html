Santorini, Greece


Santorini is a stunning Aegean island famed for its whitewashed villages, blue-domed churches, dramatic caldera views, and sunsets that turn the sky gold and rose. It blends history, romance, and seaside relaxation.

  1. Oia Sunset — the most iconic viewpoint on the island.
  2. Hike from Fira to Oia along the caldera rim.
  3. Akrotiri archaeological site — a preserved Bronze Age city.
  4. Red Beach and Perissa Black Sand shores.
  5. Catamaran cruise of the caldera and hot springs.
  • Tomatokeftedes (crispy tomato fritters).
  • Fresh grilled octopus by the harbor.
  • Fava (creamy yellow split peas) with capers.
  • Saganaki (pan-fried cheese) with lemon.
  • Loukoumades (honey-drenched doughnuts).
