Paris, France


Paris blends grand boulevards, world-class art, and riverside charm—an ideal destination for history, romance, and cafés.

  1. Eiffel Tower for sweeping city views.
  2. Louvre Museum with masterpieces like the Mona Lisa.
  3. Cruise the Seine River at sunset.
  4. Wander Montmartre and the Sacré-Cœur steps.
  5. See Notre-Dame and Île de la Cité.
  • Buttery croissants.
  • Fresh baguette sandwiches.
  • Street-side crêpes.
  • Colorful macarons.
  • Classic French onion soup.
